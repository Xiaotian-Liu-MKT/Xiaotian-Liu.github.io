### Summary
**paper-feed** helps researchers subscribe to journal feeds, filter updates, and save favorites for ongoing reading.

### Key Features
*   **Subscriptions**: Follow journals and receive new-issue alerts.
*   **Filtering**: Filter by keywords, topics, or outlets.
*   **Favorites**: Save and organize articles for later reading.
