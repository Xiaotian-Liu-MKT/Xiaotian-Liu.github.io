Provided specialized academic support for La Trobe University students during the critical transition to remote learning necessitated by the COVID-19 pandemic.

### Courses Supported
*   **BUS3DIG Digital Business**: Focused on digital transformation, e-commerce models, and digital marketing strategies.
*   **ECO3CCE Climate Change Economics and Policy**: Supported students in understanding the economic impacts of climate change and the policy mechanisms for mitigation.

### Remote Teaching Excellence
*   **Adaptive Delivery**: Successfully pivoted from traditional tutoring to a blend of synchronous (live Zoom sessions) and asynchronous (video explainers and discussion boards) delivery.
*   **Student Engagement**: Utilized digital whiteboards and interactive polling to maintain high levels of engagement in a virtual environment.
*   **Crisis Support**: Provided critical academic and emotional support to students struggling with the sudden shift in learning environments, ensuring continuity in their academic progress.
