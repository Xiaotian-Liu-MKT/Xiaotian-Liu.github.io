Served as a Teaching Assistant for the School of Business and Management, supporting core curriculum delivery for undergraduate students.

### Courses Supported
*   **Introduction to Management**: Assisted in teaching fundamental management principles, organizational behavior, and strategic planning.
*   **International Marketing**: Supported sessions on global market entry strategies, cross-cultural consumer behavior, and international branding.

### Key Contributions
*   **Material Development**: Developed comprehensive slide decks, case study summaries, and interactive classroom activities that enhanced student engagement.
*   **Assessment & Feedback**: Managed grading for mid-semester assignments and provided detailed, constructive feedback to over 100 students.
*   **Student Consultation**: Held weekly office hours to assist students with complex concepts and career guidance, fostering a supportive learning environment.
